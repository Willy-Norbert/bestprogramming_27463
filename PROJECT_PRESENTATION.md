- âŒ **Manual Booking Processes**: Time-consuming paper-based or spreadsheet systems
- âŒ **Double Bookings**: No real-time conflict detection leading to scheduling conflicts
- âŒ **Limited Visibility**: Students and staff cannot easily check resource availability
- âŒ **Inefficient Management**: Administrators struggle to track usage and generate reports
